Academia Connect – Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

Welcome to Academia Connect. We are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring transparency in how your data is handled throughout your academic networking experience.

This Privacy Policy explains:

  • What data we collect and why.
  • How we use and share your data.
  • The security measures protecting your information.
  • Your rights and control over your data.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you sign up or use Academia Connect, we collect:

  • Full Name & Email: For identity verification and communication.
  • Profile Picture: Used to personalize your profile.
  • Account Role: Student, alumni, or faculty.
  • User ID: A unique identifier generated by our system.

1.2 Academic & Networking Information

To enhance alumni–student interactions, we collect:

  • Educational Details: Course, department, year of study, skills, and interests.
  • Shared Stories & Posts: Alumni success stories, user posts, or uploaded content.
  • Marketplace Listings: Items you post for buying, selling, or exchanging.
  • Task Data: Tasks, deadlines, and events created in the app.

1.3 Communication Data

  • Messages: Chats between students and alumni.
  • Group Interactions: Group discussions, comments, and shared files.
  • Notifications: Alerts related to mentorship, tasks, or marketplace activity.

1.4 Usage Data

  • Login Activity: Dates and times of app usage.
  • Feature Interactions: How users interact with app features.
  • Error Logs: Used to diagnose technical issues.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used to provide and improve the services within Academia Connect:

  • Networking & Mentorship: Connecting students with alumni based on interests and expertise.
  • Marketplace Operations: Managing listings, transactions, and communication between buyers and sellers.
  • Task & Event Management: Helping students organize tasks, deadlines, and reminders.
  • App Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to enhance user experience.
  • Security & Verification: Ensuring safe interactions between users.

3. Data Security

We implement strong technical and administrative measures to safeguard your data:

  • Secure Authentication: Encrypted login and token-based access.
  • Encrypted Data Transfer: All communication uses HTTPS.
  • Cloud Protection: Data stored on secure, encrypted servers.
  • Role-Based Access Control: Restricted access for sensitive information.

4. Your Rights

  • Access & Edit: Update your profile or academic details anytime.
  • Delete Account: Request full deletion of your data.
  • Control Privacy: Manage who can view your profile or posts.
  • Opt-Out: Control notifications and communication settings.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google Authentication: For secure login.
  • Cloud Hosting Providers: For secure storage and backend processes.
  • AI & Analytics Tools: Used to power recommendations and improve app features.

6. Children's Privacy

Academia Connect is intended for college-level students and alumni. We do not knowingly collect information from users under the age of 13.

7. Updates to This Policy

This policy may be updated periodically. Major updates will be communicated via email or in-app notifications.
